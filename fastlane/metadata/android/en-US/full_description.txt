SecPal helps private security companies run daily operations with less paperwork and clearer field coordination.

Use SecPal to support guard teams with structured operational workflows, mobile access, and secure account handling.

Key capabilities:
- mobile access to the SecPal workspace on Android
- secure sign-in with passkeys and hardened native session handling
- support for Android rollout and managed-device scenarios
- direct APK distribution and controlled update channels where required
- a product built for German private security services and their operational reality

SecPal is designed for professional use. Availability of individual workflows depends on your SecPal deployment and your organization's permissions.
